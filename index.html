Welcome to My Computer Science Capstone ePortfolio

This ePortfolio showcases my growth throughout the Computer Science program at Southern New Hampshire University and highlights the knowledge, skills, and experience I developed through coursework, projects, and my Capstone enhancement project.

Throughout the program, I gained experience in software engineering, database design, algorithms and data structures, software testing, system analysis and design, and computer graphics. These experiences helped me strengthen both my technical abilities and my understanding of the software development process, from planning and design through implementation, testing, and documentation.

The centerpiece of this portfolio is my CS499 Capstone project, which focused on enhancing a Weight Tracker mobile application originally developed in CS360: Mobile Architecture and Programming. Through software engineering, algorithms and data structures, and database enhancements, I transformed the original application into a more robust and feature-rich solution while demonstrating the skills developed throughout the Computer Science program.

This portfolio includes my professional self-assessment, code review, artifact enhancements, technical documentation, resume, and final project deliverables. Together, these materials demonstrate my progression as a computer science student and provide evidence of the knowledge and skills I have developed throughout the program.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio. I invite you to explore the projects and artifacts presented here and learn more about my journey through the Computer Science program.
